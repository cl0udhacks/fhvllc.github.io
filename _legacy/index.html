About Us

Who We Are

Fairy Hill Ventures is a technology services company specializing in cloud engineering and security, where innovation meets excellence. We aim to empower enterprises in the cloud era with assurance and protection. We strive to stay ahead of cloud security innovations, equipping organizations with the necessary tools to secure their online resources and unlock their full potential. By leveraging advanced solutions and expertise, we aim to be the go-to ally for overcoming cyber vulnerabilities and creating a more secure internet..

What We Do

Governance, Risk & Compliance(GRC): Complete Security and compliance assessments with customized reports, policies, and procedures tailored to your organization. Standards and frameworks we use to help our customers include but are not limited to NIST and FISMA, PCI DSS, HIPAA, GDPR, and ISO/IEC 27001.

Security Audits and Risk Assessments: Need information security risk assessment to meet security compliance? We help our customers conduct security audits as well as establish risk assessment frameworks and conduct risk assessments using MAS TRM, ISO/IEC 27005, NIST, and more.!
